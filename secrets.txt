Their are cheats in this game.

You can use them by entering secret keys.

Here are the keys:

# Pc Keys:
- Use "shift + h" to toggle on or off hitboxes.
- Use "h" to show hitboxes.

# Mobile Keys:
- Hold for 6 seconds to toggle on or off hitboxes.
- Tap 20 times (fast or it wouldn't work) to show hitboxes.
